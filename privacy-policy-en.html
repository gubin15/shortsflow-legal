ShortsFlow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShortsFlow ("the App", "we", "us", "our"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in via Google OAuth, we collect your Google account email, username, and profile picture
  • YouTube Channel Information: Channel ID, channel name, subscriber count, and other public information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • YouTube Video Data: Video titles, descriptions, view counts, likes, comments, publish dates obtained through YouTube Data API
  • Usage Data: App feature usage, operation logs (stored locally only)
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version (for app performance optimization)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your YouTube password
  • We do NOT collect payment information
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT collect contact information

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for:

  • Core Features: Video management, data analysis, statistics reporting
  • Smart Insights: Analyzing video performance, providing optimization suggestions
  • User Experience: Improving app performance and features
  • AI Features: Using Google Gemini AI to generate content suggestions (optional)

4. Information Storage

4.1 Local Storage

  • All video data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device
  • Login credentials (tokens) are stored encrypted in device secure storage
  • App settings and preferences are stored locally

4.2 Cloud Storage

  • We do NOT upload your data to our servers
  • All data remains on your device only

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google OAuth

  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.2 YouTube Data API

  • Purpose: Retrieving YouTube video data
  • Permissions: Read-only, cannot modify your YouTube content
  • Terms: YouTube Terms of Service

5.3 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Generating content optimization suggestions (optional)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

6. Data Security

  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive information is encrypted
  • HTTPS: All network requests use HTTPS encryption
  • Access Control: Only you can access your data
  • Regular Updates: Security patches applied promptly

7. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data in the app anytime
  • Export: Export your data (CSV, JSON, Excel)
  • Delete: Clear all local data in settings
  • Revoke: Revoke Google account authorization anytime

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we've collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you in the app. Continued use indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
